Smash burger

A crispy-edged, juicy burger smashed flat on the griddle for maximum flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80/20)
  • Salt & pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles
  • Butter for buns

Steps

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet or griddle on high heat.
  2. Divide beef into 4 equal balls.
  3. Place one ball on the hot surface and smash it down firmly with a spatula.
  4. Cook 2-3 minutes until edges are crispy, then flip.
  5. Add cheese slice immediately if using; cook another minute.
  6. Toast buns with butter on the skillet.
  7. Assemble burger with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and patties.

Crispy Chicken Wings

Perfectly crispy, spicy wings with a hint of honey and paprika.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Mix hot sauce, honey, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss wings in the sauce mixture.
  4. Arrange wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake 25-30 minutes until crispy and cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with extra sauce if desired.

Chicago Deep Dish Pizza

Thick crust pizza loaded with cheese, tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, sausage, veggies)

Steps

  1. Activate yeast in warm water with sugar; let foam for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Mix flour and salt, add yeast mixture and oil; knead dough for 5 minutes.
  3. Let dough rise 1 hour in a warm place.
  4. Press dough into a deep dish pizza pan.
  5. Layer cheese first, then toppings, then cover with crushed tomatoes.
  6. Bake at 220°C (425°F) for 30-35 minutes until crust is golden.
  7. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Homemade Pasta

Fresh, hearty pasta perfect with your favorite sauce.

Ingredients

  • 300g all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour and salt on a clean surface.
  2. Make a well in the center and crack in eggs and oil.
  3. Gradually mix flour into eggs until dough forms.
  4. Knead dough for 10 minutes until smooth.
  5. Rest dough covered for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll out and cut into desired pasta shapes.
  7. Cook in boiling salted water 2-3 minutes until al dente.

Lasagna

Layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package, drain.
  2. Brown ground beef; add marinara sauce and simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Spread a layer of meat sauce in baking dish.
  4. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat.
  5. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 45 minutes covered, then 15 uncovered.
  7. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Fluffy Pancakes

Light and fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake; cook until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and cook another 2 minutes until golden.
  7. Serve with syrup or toppings.

Caesar Salad

Crisp romaine tossed in creamy Caesar dressing with croutons and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Chop romaine lettuce and place in a large bowl.
  2. Add Caesar dressing and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Add croutons and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Toss gently and serve immediately.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic chewy cookies loaded with chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well.
  4. Mix in flour and baking soda.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet.
  7. Bake 10-12 minutes until edges are golden.

Greek Salad

Fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and feta with a lemon dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup olives
  • 100g feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, and olives in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Toss gently and crumble feta on top.
  4. Serve chilled.

Chicken Curry

Spicy, creamy curry simmered with tender chicken pieces.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thighs, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot; sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chicken and brown all sides.
  3. Stir in curry powder and cook 1 minute.
  4. Add coconut milk and simmer 20 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Season with salt and serve with rice.

French Toast

Golden, buttery slices of bread soaked in egg custard and fried.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Butter for frying

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices in the mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook bread until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve warm with syrup or fruit.

Guacamole

Creamy avocado dip with lime and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl.
  2. Add lime juice, tomato, onion, cilantro, and salt.
  3. Mix well and adjust seasoning.
  4. Serve with chips or as a topping.
